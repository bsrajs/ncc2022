Paper Submission Details


Authors are encouraged to use LATEX to format their final manuscripts, using the standard IEEE conference specifications. pdflatex is the preferred way of generating the PDF file. Please ensure the paper size if you use other programs.

Final manuscripts should not exceed SIX pages in length, should use font size no smaller than 10points, and have margins on all four sides as per IEEE conference format. Papers should have no page numbers and no headers or footers. All fonts should be embedded in the pdf file. The system will not accept the upload of final manuscripts longer than SIX pages. Final papers accepted for publcation should have a minimum length of THREE pages.

Best paper awards will be given as in the previous editions, details to follow.

Your manuscript submission must be uploaded via EDAS. Note that this is a separate website. No upload facility is available on the NCC 2022 site. Some general guidelines to prepare your manuscripts are listed below.
