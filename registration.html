Registration


We will start the registration after the paper submission deadline is over. The early registration discount applies only till January 15th, 2015. The registration charges are,

Type Charges
Full-Registration Before January 15th, 2015 Rs 10,000
Student-Registration Before January 15th, 2015 Rs 4,000
Full-Registration after January 15th, 2015 Rs 12,000
Student-Registration after January 15th, 2015 Rs 6,000

For authors, one registration covers for uploading three accepted papers.

Several travel fellowships will be made available for the student participants. Their exact number and support will be announced after 15th January 2015.

For any queries, please contact the General Chairs of NCC 2015.
