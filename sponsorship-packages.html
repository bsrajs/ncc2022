Sponsorship Benefits


The twenty-first edition of the National Conference on Communications, NCC 2015 offers a unique opportunity to showcase your organization as an industry leader in India as well as globally. This premier Indian conference, organized by the joint telematics group of the IITs and IISc in the areas of communications and signal processing, owes its reputation to a long tradition as well as wide participation from the industry as well as academia.

Sponsors of NCC2015 can showcase their service, technology and solutions to an august gathering of the foremost communication technologists in India. The conference also provides extensive networking opportunities, which can be fully utilized by availing the complementary registrations awarded to the sponsors.

Sponsorship Categories

The following categories of sponsorship packages are available to suit your needs at NCC2015.

a) Platinum Sponsor (Investment: Rs. 500000/-)

Name of the sponsor as well as its logo will appear in all documents including banner on the IITB Main Gate, poster, hoarding, and proceedings, of the conference. Sponsors will be provided space at two places of the conference venue to showcase their products. Acknowledgment of sponsor at start/end of conference. Five complementary conference registrations for the sponsor's staff is included with the package.

b) Gold Sponsor (Investment: Rs. 200000/-)

Name of the sponsor as well as its logo will appear in all documents including banner (except the banner on the IITB Main Gate), poster, hoarding, and proceedings, of the conference. Sponsors will be provided space at a strategic place of the conference venue to showcase their products. Acknowledgment of sponsor at start/end of conference. Two complementary conference registrations for the sponsor's staff is included with the package.

c) Silver Sponsor (Investment: Rs. 100000/-)

The sponsors name will appear in the banner and posters of the main conference hall where the plenary talks take place. Sponsors will be provided a single space to showcase their products. 10percent discount on the conference registration fees for the sponsor's staff is included with this package.

d) Bronze Sponsor (Investment: Rs. 50000/-)

The sponsor's name will appear in the main conference room and will be acknowledged during the event inauguration.
